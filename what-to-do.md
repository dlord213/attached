# Attached

An app for couple.

### Phase 1 (Features)

- Authentication/Authorization (Login/Register/Forgot Password/Forgot Email)
- Share Live Status
- Share Current Location
- Share Notes/Tasks/Calendar Schedules
- Shared Gallery
- Shared Quest Board
- "On this Day" Time Capsule
- Mood Journaling
- "Low Battery" Alerts
- Blind Choice (Swipe Match from stack of 10 local restaurants or 10 movies from a shared watchlist)
- Shared Watchlist (Movies/TV Shows/Anime/Music)
- Love Notes (Home Widget)
- Monthly Recap (AI-generated summary of the month's shared activities and memories)
- Milestone Countdown (Anniversaries, Birthdays, etc.)
- Do Not Disturb (DND) sync
- Shared Reading List
- Shared Pet/Plant or any entity
- Emergency Button (A one-tap alert that sends the user's live location, battery percentage, and a "Call me now" request to the partner, bypassing any "Do Not Disturb" settings)
- Shared Bucket List
- Shared "What to Do" (Blind Choice)
- Document Vault or Document "Safety Deposit Box" (An encrypted space for photos of things you always lose: the Wi-Fi password, the spare key location, insurance cards, or the lease agreement)
- Current location widget (shows partner location)
- Shared Doodle Widget
- Relationship "Time Machine" (A "Random Memory" button. Tapping it surfaces a random photo, note, or completed task from a random date in your history to spark a "Remember when?" conversation)
- Shared Habit Tracker
- The "Battery Buddy" (If your partner’s phone is under 5%, the app allows you to "Pounce" on their location one last time or sends you their last known coordinates automatically before the phone dies)
- Shared Clipboard (A "Copy on my phone, Paste on yours" feature. Great for sharing links, addresses, or long snippets of text instantly)
